    Airo AI Builder FAQ

    What is Airo AI Builder?

    Airo AI Builder is an AI-powered platform for entrepreneurs, solopreneurs, and developers who want to build a site, app, game, or store — not just a website — from a conversation. You can also build the backend of your web app or website. Describe what you want in any language; Airo builds it. No coding, no learning curve. Branding, security, database, and marketing included. It's fully integrated into the GoDaddy ecosystem, so you get world-class support and reliable hosting from a brand you trust. Currently Airo AI Builder is available in 150+ countries with multi-language support.

    Do I need coding experience?

    No. Airo AI Builder is a no-code web app builder designed for all skill levels — from first-time builders with no technical background to experienced developers who want to move faster. Airo immerses you in vibe coding experience and lets you build intuitively, with natural language.

    What can I build with Airo AI Builder?

    Anything from landing pages and portfolios to e-commerce stores, booking systems, marketplaces, client portals, and business dashboards. If you can describe it, Airo can build it—no coding experience required on your part.

    How is Airo AI Builder different from other no-code tools?

    Airo works with natural language and generates custom full-stack solutions, not just templates. Unlike standalone vibe coding tools or no-code platforms, Airo AI Builder includes hosting, payments, auth, and deployment in one place — so you go from idea to live business without stitching tools together. Plus, it's fully integrated into the GoDaddy ecosystem, meaning you get world-class support, seamless domain connection, and reliable hosting from a brand you trust.

    Can I see and edit the code Airo generates?

    Yes. While Airo is a no-code app builder, we don’t lock you out. You can view the generated code and make manual edits if you want to fine-tune things directly.

    Does Airo AI Builder support mobile apps?

    Airo AI Builder focuses on web applications, but you can build mobile-responsive web apps that work great on any device.

    Can I use a custom domain?

    Yes. You can connect your own domain to any published project.

    Can I publish and host my project through Airo AI Builder?

    Yes. Hosting and deployment are included with each plan, so you can publish directly from our online AI builder.

    Can I connect my own data and APIs?

    Yes. Airo AI Builder includes built-in database and backend capabilities, so your app can store data and manage users. You can also integrate third-party development tools and services like Stripe, analytics, or custom APIs just by asking Airo to connect them in your prompt.

    How does pricing work?

    Airo AI Builder uses a plan-based model. You can start for free and upgrade as your needs grow. Each plan includes a set of credits that power your building and app operations. If you run out of credits, you can buy additional credits or wait until your next credit cycle resets. Credits expire at the end of each billing cycle and do not roll over to the next cycle.

    Who owns the code and projects I create?

    You do. Everything you build with Airo AI Builder belongs to you.

    Is my data secure?

    Yes. Airo AI Builder follows industry-standard best practices for data protection, privacy, and security.
