# Dental Clinic Research And Website Category Decision Prompt

You are researching a dental clinic before building a professional one-page website.

## Clinic Information

Clinic Name:
Dr. Annu's Dental Care

Clinic Address or City:
Plot No. 5, Gangadevi Nagar,
Near HP Petrol Pump, Radisson Service Road,
Vijay Nagar, Indore 452010

## Research Instructions

Research the clinic using publicly available sources.

Research priority order:

1. Google Business Profile / Google Maps
2. Official clinic website (if available)
3. Justdial
4. Practo
5. Lybrate, myUpchar, KiViHealth, or similar listing platforms
6. Social media profiles (only if additional information is needed)

## Information To Collect

Collect only verified information:

* Clinic name
* Doctor name and qualifications
* Services offered
* Address and city
* Contact number
* Ratings and review count
* Operating hours
* Review themes and patient sentiment
* Clinic positioning (doctor-led, family-oriented, cosmetic-focused, etc.)
* Missing or unavailable information

## Website Category Decision

Decide which website category best fits the clinic.

### Categories

1. Neighborhood

* Small doctor-led clinic
* Personal/local patient base
* Lower digital presence
* Simple and approachable positioning

2. Neighborhood+

* Doctor-led and warm
* Better branding and stronger ratings
* Broader services and polished presentation
* City-level reach

3. Family

* Established or multi-doctor clinic
* Family/general dentistry focus
* Strong review presence
* Structured and trust-oriented care

4. Premium/Cosmetic

* Smile design, veneers, whitening, Invisalign, implants
* Premium branding
* Aesthetic/result-focused positioning
* Editorial/luxury presentation style

## Important Rules

* Do not invent facts.
* Do not generate fake ratings, reviews, qualifications, or experience.
* If a fact is unavailable, clearly mention it as missing.
* Explain how future website copy should avoid depending on unavailable information.
* Do not use placeholders in recommendations.
* Keep locality references limited to contact/address sections where appropriate.
* The website should feel suitable for city-level reach, not only nearby local traffic.
